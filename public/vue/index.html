Vue

Vue’s declarative, HTML-first syntax makes it so enjoyable, friendly and at the same time efficient to work with it.

As Angular 2 was such a breaking change from Angular 1, the situation was a bit confusing for a time and brought me and my team to explore new tools. While React’s “it’s just JavaScript” approach makes it very flexible, Vue rather follows the rule of least power by sticking closer to the HTML/templating mindset, which makes it most suitable and inclusive for web-inclined people.
